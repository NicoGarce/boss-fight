# How to Win Boss Fight: Step-by-Step Instructions

## Step 1: Choose Your Career
1. Select a career from the dropdown menu
2. Any career works - choose one you're familiar with for easier special questions
3. Click "Start Interview"

## Step 2: Level 1 - Resume Screening (Easy)

**Your Goal**: Reduce interviewer HP to 0

**What to do**:
1. Wait for the interviewer to ask a question
2. Click "Buzzword Attack" (costs 20 energy)
3. Repeat "Buzzword Attack" until interviewer HP is low
4. If your Confidence drops significantly (below 1/3), use "Confidence Boost"
5. Keep attacking until interviewer HP reaches 0
6. Popup will show "Interview Passed!" - wait 2 seconds for auto-dismiss
7. Level 2 begins automatically

**Don't use**: Panic Dodge (not needed for easy questions)

## Step 3: Level 2 - HR Interview (Medium)

**Your Goal**: Reduce interviewer HP to 0

**What to do**:
1. Check your Luck stat (shown on your card)
2. If Luck is 25 or higher: Use "Portfolio Flex" (30 energy) for chance at critical hit
3. If Luck is below 25: Use "Buzzword Attack" (20 energy) for consistent damage
4. When you see a "medium" difficulty question: Use "Panic Dodge" (15 energy)
5. Keep your Confidence healthy (don't let it get too low)
6. If Confidence drops significantly: Use "Confidence Boost" (20 energy)
7. Keep attacking until interviewer HP reaches 0
8. Wait for popup to auto-dismiss
9. Level 3 begins automatically

**Energy Tip**: Energy regenerates 10 points each turn - don't worry about running out

## Step 4: Level 3 - Skills/Technical Test (Hard)

**Your Goal**: Reduce interviewer HP to 0

**What to do**:
1. Start with "Panic Dodge" (15 energy) - questions are harder now
2. Use "Portfolio Flex" (30 energy) for damage
3. If your HP drops significantly (below half): Use "Heal" (25 energy)
4. If your Confidence drops significantly: Use "Confidence Boost" (20 energy)
5. Use "Panic Dodge" frequently on "hard" difficulty questions
6. Keep both HP and Confidence healthy
7. Keep attacking until interviewer HP reaches 0
8. Wait for popup to auto-dismiss
9. Level 4 begins automatically

**Critical**: This is the hardest level - be aggressive with healing and confidence boosting

## Step 5: Level 4 - Manager Interview (Hard)

**Your Goal**: Reduce interviewer HP to 0 OR answer special question

**INSTANT WIN METHOD**:
1. Wait for a special question to appear
2. A golden "Answer" button will appear on your action panel
3. Click the "Answer" button
4. Type: `O(log n)` or `log n`
5. Press Enter
6. Interviewer defeated instantly!
7. Wait for popup to auto-dismiss
8. Level 5 begins automatically

**IF YOU MISS THE SPECIAL QUESTION**:
1. Use "Panic Dodge" on hard questions to reduce damage
2. Keep Confidence healthy (don't let it get too low)
3. Use "Heal" when HP drops significantly
4. Use "Portfolio Flex" for damage
5. Fight normally until interviewer HP reaches 0

**Note**: The special question is the same for ALL careers: "What is the time complexity of binary search?"

## Step 6: Level 5 - Final Interview (Expert)

**Your Goal**: Reduce interviewer HP to 0 OR answer your career's special question

**INSTANT WIN METHOD**:
1. Wait for your career's special question to appear
2. A golden "Answer" button will appear
3. Click the "Answer" button
4. Type the answer based on your career (see below)
5. Press Enter
6. Interviewer defeated instantly!
7. Congratulations - you won!

**SPECIAL QUESTION ANSWERS BY CAREER**:
- **Professional Sleeper, Dog Surfing, Ice Cream Taster, etc.**: Wait for the "O(log n)" question (same as Level 4)
- **Software Developer**: "What is the time complexity of binary search?" → Type: `O(log n)`
- **Frontend Developer**: "What is the purpose of the CSS box-sizing property?" → Type: `border-box`
- **Backend Developer**: "What does ACID stand for in databases?" → Type: `Atomicity, Consistency, Isolation, Durability`
- **DevOps Engineer**: "What is a container in DevOps?" → Type: `A lightweight, standalone package`
- **Full Stack Developer**: "What is the difference between monolithic and microservices?" → Type: `Monolithic is single unit, microservices are distributed`

**IF YOU MISS THE SPECIAL QUESTION**:
1. Use all your tools: Heal, Confidence Boost, Panic Dodge
2. Keep both HP and Confidence healthy
3. Use "Portfolio Flex" for damage
4. Use "Panic Dodge" on expert difficulty questions
5. Fight normally until interviewer HP reaches 0

## What to Do If You Lose

**When your Confidence reaches 0**:
1. A popup will appear with game over message
2. Click "Return to Menu" to go back to career selection
3. OR click "Try Again" to restart the current level with same career

**Tips for retrying**:
- Use "Try Again" instead of "Return to Menu" to restart current level
- Review which level you struggled with
- Be more aggressive with healing and confidence boosting

## Quick Reference Card

### Action Costs:
- Buzzword Attack: 20 energy
- Portfolio Flex: 30 energy
- Panic Dodge: 15 energy
- Heal: 25 energy (3 turn cooldown)
- Confidence Boost: 20 energy (2 turn cooldown)

### When to Heal:
- When HP drops significantly (below half)
- Remember the 3-turn cooldown - plan ahead

### When to Boost Confidence:
- When Confidence drops significantly (below 1/3)
- Remember the 2-turn cooldown - use it before it gets critical

### When to Use Panic Dodge:
- On "hard" or "expert" difficulty questions
- When you expect high damage
- Before the interviewer speaks

### Special Question Answers (Memorize These):
- Binary search time complexity: `O(log n)`
- CSS box-sizing: `border-box`
- ACID in databases: `Atomicity, Consistency, Isolation, Durability`
- Container in DevOps: `A lightweight, standalone package`
- Monolithic vs microservices: `Monolithic is single unit, microservices are distributed`

## Summary: The Winning Formula

1. **Levels 1-2**: Attack aggressively, use Confidence Boost when Confidence drops significantly
2. **Level 3**: Use Panic Dodge frequently, heal when HP drops significantly, boost confidence when needed
3. **Level 4**: Wait for special question, answer `O(log n)` for instant win (or fight normally if you miss it)
4. **Level 5**: Wait for your career's special question, answer correctly for instant win (or fight normally if you miss it)
5. **If you lose**: Click "Try Again" to restart current level

**Key Strategy**: Since damage is random, react to the situation - heal when HP is low, boost confidence when it's low, use panic dodge on hard questions. Don't rely on fixed HP numbers - watch your bars and react accordingly.

That's it! Follow these steps and you'll win the game.
